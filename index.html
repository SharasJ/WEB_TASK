About Us

As a member of Golden Tennis Club you will have unlimited use of the courts and facilities. Floodlight tokens may be purchased from committee members for $5 per hour for 2 courts. We run 2 club evenings a week, Mondays and Wednesdays, along with an informal mix-in session on Thursday mornings from 10.30am-12.30pm. Everyone is welcome to turn up and join in. Visitors are welcome for a nominal $2 guest fee and are welcome to play up to 3 times before being asked to join. Becoming a member could not be easier. Please browse the website or come along to a club session on a Monday or Wednesday evening between 6.30 and 8.30pm and talk to the committee member on duty who will be pleased to give you all the information you need. Remember to bring your tennis racket, so you can have a game. Alternatively you can contact the Club Secretary – info@goldentennis.club or more details on contacts page.
